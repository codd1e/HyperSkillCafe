Our Products

Latte

A coffee drink originally from Italy, consisting of milk and espresso coffee. Latte is brewed on the basis of milk, forming a three-layer mixture of milk, espresso and foam in a cup or glass

Capucinno

Coffee drink of Italian cuisine based on espresso with the addition of warmed foamed milk

Raf

A coffee drink consisting of cream, vanilla sugar or syrup and espresso coffee.

Mocco

Mocco is a type of Arabica coffee, named after the port city of Moha in Yemen. This is an espresso-based drink with the addition of milk, cocoa powder or chocolate syrup

Bicerin

Espresso-based drink with the addition of bitter chocolate and cream. Translated from Italian, "Bicerin" is a "small glass".

Espresso

Espresso is a method of making coffee by passing hot water under pressure through a filter with ground coffee.
